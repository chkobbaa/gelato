Our Story

A Family Tradition of Sweet Perfection

Born in the sun-kissed hills of Tuscany in 1987, Gelato Dreams began as a small family cart on the cobblestone streets of Florence. Our founder, Nonna Maria, believed that gelato wasn't just a dessert — it was a way to make people smile.

Three generations later, we still use her original recipes, sourcing the finest ingredients from local farmers and bringing that same warmth and joy to every scoop we serve.

Our Craft

How We Make Magic

Every scoop tells a story of passion, patience, and perfection.

1
🌾

Source the Finest

Each morning, we handpick the freshest seasonal ingredients from local farms and trusted artisan suppliers around the world.

2
🔄

Slow Churn Perfection

Using traditional Italian batch freezers, we slowly churn small batches to create the densest, creamiest texture possible.

3
🎨

Craft with Love

Our gelato artisans fold, swirl, and layer each flavor by hand — creating unique textures and flavor profiles in every batch.
